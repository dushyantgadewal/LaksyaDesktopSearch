Privacy :

Lakshya  is committed to making search on your desktop easy. We recognize that privacy is an important issue. Hence we designed and built Lakshya Desktop configurable, so that one can use this software according to its need .

So that you can easily search your computer, the Lakshya Desktop application indexes and stores versions of your files (configurable by user). These versions may also be mixed with your Web search results to produce results pages for you that integrate relevant content from your computer and information from the Web.

Your computer's content is not made accessible to Any Site or anyone else without your explicit permission.
